UNFILTERED CRAFT CIDER

OUR STORY

Massachusetts, 1991: It started with a dream, or rather...two dreams. Two young brothers, Ross and Matt Brockman, awoke at the exact moment. Both had experienced similar visions. “Unfiltered Craft Cider!”, they both exclaimed, in unison. They were four years old and seven years old, respectively. Twenty years later, the dream became reality.

OUR CIDER

Downeast Cider is an unfiltered craft cider, made to taste just like the farm fresh apple cider our founders grew up drinking in New England. We keep our cider unfiltered for a full-bodied, farm fresh true apple cider flavor.

Flavors range from our Orginal flavor and range from Blackberry, Pear, Double Blend, and more!
